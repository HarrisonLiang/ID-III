No experience, no problem
As the population in cities grows, people have less opportunity to learn how to grow plants. Existing tools are transactional, they focus on soil fertility or moisture levels at a point in time. They all lack a larger context of how to care for plants from seed to maturity. Having more people learn how to care for plants will lead to a greener tomorrow.
How SmartGrow can help
SmartGrow connects your phone with your plants, allowing you to see live readings from its sensors. The app will cater instructions on how to care for and raise your plant based on direct readings and it will also help you plan for your plant’s future needs.
  • Monitor whats happening beneath and around your plan
  • Step by step instructions on how to raise and solve plant problems based on live readings, but it will also help you plan for your plant’s future needs..
  • Learn to raise a variety of over 5,000 plants!
